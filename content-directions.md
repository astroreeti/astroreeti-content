# Content pillars Reeti wants (fold into the 30-day calendar)

## Pillar A — Real-life question posts ("which house rules my ___")
Frame around the questions people actually ask. Each post:
- The life area (business, marriage, career, money, health, children, foreign travel, litigation, property)
- Which Bhava (house) governs it — primary + supporting houses
- Which planet(s) should be strong / well-placed for good results
- What today's / this-week's transit is doing to that house or planet
- The practical takeaway or timing implication
Tone: answer the reader's real worry, not textbook theory. "Shaadi kab hogi?" energy.

## Pillar B — Mythology & nakshatra stories
The 27 nakshatras + navagraha + rashi devtas — the STORY behind each:
- The deity/legend attached to the constellation (e.g. Rohini & Chandra, Ashwini Kumaras, Krittika & Kartikeya)
- What that myth reveals about the nakshatra's personality/energy
- Make it RELATABLE — connect the ancient story to a modern human trait or situation
  ("if your Moon is in Rohini, why you crave beauty and comfort — the Chandra-Rohini love story explains it")
Serial potential: "Nakshatra Katha" — one nakshatra per episode, 27-part series = 27 guaranteed posts.

## Note on accuracy
- Any transit/planetary-position claim MUST be verified against a sidereal panchang
  (drikpanchang.com) at generation time — never state positions from memory.
- Mythology: attribute to the tradition; present as "as per Vedic tradition / Puranic account,"
  not as literal fact, to stay respectful and accurate.
- No fear-based predictions, no guaranteed-outcome or medical/financial promises.
