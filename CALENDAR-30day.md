## Series mechanics (why this compounds)

- **Nakshatra Katha runs Days 1→27+** — a serial creates return viewers and gives
  people a reason to *follow* ("episode 2 tomorrow"). CTA each episode:
  "comment KATHA for the next one." 27 episodes = 27 unique posts nobody can copy.
- **[RL] posts end with a comment prompt** ("comment your Moon sign / your 7th-house
  sign") to drive the comment rate the algorithm rewards.
- **[DD] technical posts are the authority moat** — the white space no big account
  touches. These are the ones serious followers save and share to their groups.
- Weekly, I swap any underperforming slot for more of whatever the data rewards.
